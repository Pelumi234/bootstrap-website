When summer is in full force with the sun overhead blazing in a bleached blue sky, we convince ourselves the grey cloud cover and the dark days will never come.

Minimalissimo Selection: Architecture

A new book that caught our attention: Minimalissimo Selection is a visual exploration of architecture within the spectrum of minimalism. Jump in for a peek inside along with a short video exploring this new tome.

Sail Loft

Among other projects our studio is currently working on the designs for a new home in the sheltered port of Milford Haven, UK.

When life gives you lemons...

Embracing the idiom in the above title, we can happily conclude we made lemonade. Soaked through from a day of torrential rain and back in dry clothes from a long day and night photoshoot in Oxford. We completed the exterior catalogue for Quatrefoil House.

Silver House + Triumph Motorbikes

We were delighted to see global brand Triumph choose Silver House on the Gower Peninsula as a shoot location to capture the new limited edition motorcycle inspired by the Tiger 900 Rally Pro, which features stunt sequences in ‘No Time To Die.

Plinth House - In Process

Various plinth monoliths complete with specific functions: A plunge pool, a barbecue, stepping stones for gathering and seating cascade up from the beach to terminate in the final plinth - a platform for living, vailed under a suspended timber filagree screen.

Remarkable Places

Edge House Model
